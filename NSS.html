WHO ARE WE?

NIT RAIPUR NSS CONSISTS OF HIGHLY MOTIVATED VOLUNTEERS VOLUNTEERS WHO WANT TO CONTRIBUTE TO SOCIETY

National Service Scheme was founded in 1969 with the aim of providing the youths of the country with hands-on experience of community service. We, at NIT Raipur aim to do the same. Team NSS of NIT Raipur is committed to organizing events that help to promote social awareness and give something back to the society.
